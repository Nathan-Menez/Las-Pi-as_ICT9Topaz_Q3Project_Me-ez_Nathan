    THE LIVING TOMBSTONE

    The Living Tombstone, or TLT, is an electronic-alternative-indie rock band created by Yoav Landau. It is known for it's songs about popular games such as a Five Night's at Freddy's and it's original songs such as My Ordinary Life and Sunburn.

    ABOUT

    "The Living Tombstone is a musical project which takes elements from the electronic dance genres of today and combining it with rock genre influences. Its lyrical content is varied from original songs written based on personal experiences, to songs about content I like from different mediums such as movies, shows and video games."

    HISTORY

    It started with Yoav Landau in 2011 making remixes and some original songs about things like FNAF, My Little Pony, and other media. He eventually partnered up with Sam Haft and he served as the main vocalist for The Living Tombstone. In 2020, one of their biggest projects ever, zero_one, was made. It was the bands first original album. The songs were created by Yoav Landau, Sam Haft, Maxime Cholley, Guy Bernfeld, and Or "Orko" Cohen. Following that was zero_one:reloaded, the band's second album. A new album is currently in the works.

    REVIEW TO "IT'S BEEN SO LONG" SONG

    "You cannot argue with me that this WASN'T one of the best songs of 2014. Like it's seriously almost perfect, the beat is infectious and the melody is sticky yet really sad. Always loved the little harmonies before the chorus. The Living Tombstone knows how to make a damn great drop." (midnightbabe, 2020)
